History

40 years of history providing tasty & healthy products

1978

Foundation

- Mr. José Maicas founded Incarlesa
- The facilities were located in Mislata (Valencia, Spain)

1985

Expansion

- Incarlesa moves to Fuente del Jarro (Valencia, Spain)
-  The new facilities have 2,650m²

1994

New Image

- Introduction of the Incarlesa stars
- Combination of tradition & modernity

2004

New Microwave Products

- Incarlesa patents its Microwaves products (sausage, blood sausage, chorizo)

2017

IFS Certification

- Incarlesa obtains a level HIGH score


40th Anniversary

In 2018 Incarlesa celebrates 40 years providing top quality products.
