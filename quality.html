Quality

Quality is a top priority for Incarlesa. We ensure top quality throughout all our processes, from the purchase of raw material to the customer delivery.

1. Raw Material

2. Production System

3. Quality checks

1. Raw material

Incarlesa purchases top quality raw material from local Spanish ranchers. We proudly mantain our supplier base since 1978, ensuring a consistent and recognised taste.



2. Production system

Food Safety is guaranteed through a rigorous control at all manufacturing processes. Our quality System is focused on satisfaction of customer needs & search for continuous improvement.


3. Quality checks

All Incarlesa products go through exhaustive quality checks to ensure tasty, healthy and safe products. The company has a Laboratory, where daily microbiological analysis are carried out.


4. Delivery

Incarlesa offers safe & fresh transportation to all the world by boat, plane or truck.

Certifications

IFS


This quality and food safety standard is published by food distribution groups. Any company that wants to supply its food products to such distributors must comply with these standards. Distributors require an independent third party to approve the quality and food safety system of their suppliers.

ISO 9001



International standard related to quality management, applicable to any organization from all types of business sectors and activities.
